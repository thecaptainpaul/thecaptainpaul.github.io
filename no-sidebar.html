Hello there !

I'm Oki Ayokunle Paul

I see the world differently since I am a data analyst and a photographer. While my love of photography motivates me to capture the beauty and complexity of everyday life, my analytical abilities enable me to tackle problems with a logical, data-driven perspective. Data visualization, statistical analysis, machine learning, and data querying are some of my areas of expertise in my work as a data analyst. I've worked in the commercial, media technology, and educational sectors for more than 3 years. I am vast in a range of analytical and statistical tools including; Microsoft Excel, Power Bi, Tableau, SQL and Python .
